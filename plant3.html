Ever wonder what a plant could be as a font? No? Well now you're going to find out!

Louisiana Yucca

Courier New

Monotype, etc.

Also known as the Gulf Coast yucca, these plants are evergreen and resilient. Like other yucca species, it can only be pollinated by yucca moths, which may or may not be found close by.

Courier New was specifically developed for electronic use, where it remains primarily in spaces of programming alongside other monospace typefaces. It reflects the continuity and specified nature of the yucca.
