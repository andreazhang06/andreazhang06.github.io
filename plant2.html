Plant Name

Coelus

Font Name

Rockwell

Designer Name

Monotype

Originally from Southeast Asia and Oceania, coleus leaves have a wide range of vibrant colors, from green to purple to orange, that make it ideal for ornament.

Rockwell's uniform thickness makes it most ideal for display in large sizes, a contrast to the array of colors found in the coleus. Interestingly, both are still primarily used for decoration thanks to these characteristics.
